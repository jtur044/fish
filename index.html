Jumping Fish

A simple example of a "cardiff fish". You can change values before starting the expeirment. The thickness of the outline is the visual acuity. You should notice that the fish outline quality deteriorates for small logMAR VA (i.e., good VA). This depends on the screen quality. Note that increasing distance allows better VA to be shown.
